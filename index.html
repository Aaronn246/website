A Discord bot that does it all! It provides a large amount of free

Features

Moderation

Don’t stress yourself out when dealing with misbehaving members. By enabling Vertz’s high-quality moderation, you’ll have them disciplined in no time!

Leveling

Like other Discord bots, Vertz has a leveling feature that saves a member’s messaging data. The more they engage with others, the higher their levels go.

Reliability
